Privacy Policy for Pixs

Last updated: August 7, 2024

Introduction

Pixs is a photo discovery app that allows users to browse and download high-quality images from Unsplash. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • App preferences and settings
  • Favorite images and collections
  • Search queries

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

Third-Party Services

  • Unsplash API: We use Unsplash's API to fetch images. Unsplash may collect usage data as per their privacy policy.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase for analytics and remote configuration.
  • OpenAI: We use OpenAI's API for image generation features.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Analyze app performance
  • Send important updates

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (Firebase, Unsplash, OpenAI)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt out of analytics
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes.
